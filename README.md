# **Family Business Governance Platform**

Arabic digital platform developed to support governance practices in family businesses. It provides a centralized and structured environment for managing internal regulations, organizing voting processes, monitoring compliance, and improving communication among stakeholders. The platform is designed to make governance more efficient, transparent, and accessible through a modern digital experience.

## Background
Family businesses often face challenges in governance due to informal processes, limited role clarity, inconsistent regulation management, and a lack of structured communication. These challenges may affect decision-making quality, compliance, and organizational stability. Sila was proposed as a digital solution to address these issues by offering a unified platform tailored to the governance needs of family-owned businesses.

## Purpose
The purpose of Sila is to provide family businesses with a practical and user-friendly platform that enables them to:

- Manage governance regulations and internal policies digitally
- Organize and facilitate voting on important decisions
- Provide role-based access for different stakeholders
- Monitor governance compliance through a dedicated index
- Improve transparency, clarity, and internal collaboration

## Target Users
Sila is designed for the main participants involved in family business governance, including:

- **Admin**
- **Shareholder**
- **Independent Director**

## Key Features

### 1. Arabic RTL Interface
The platform is fully designed in Arabic with right-to-left layout support to ensure a natural, clear, and accessible experience for Arabic-speaking users.

### 2. Secure Authentication
Sila includes a secure authentication system that allows users to sign up and log in based on their assigned roles. Each role is linked to specific permissions and dashboard access.

### 3. Role-Based Dashboards
The platform provides a customized dashboard for each user role:

- **Admin:** can monitor total regulations, total users, and pending votes
- **Shareholder:** can review active regulations, personal voting records, and the family charter
- **Independent Director:** can access regulations and recommendations relevant to governance processes

### 4. Regulations Management
A dedicated section is provided for viewing, organizing, and managing governance regulations and internal business policies in a structured and accessible format.

### 5. Voting System
Sila supports digital voting processes that allow stakeholders to participate in important decisions in a clear, organized, and transparent manner.

### 6. Members Management
The platform includes a members page that presents relevant information about shareholders and participants within the governance structure.

### 7. Compliance Index
A dedicated compliance index page is included to help monitor and evaluate governance and regulatory compliance within the family business.

### 8. Settings
Users are able to manage their account preferences and personal settings through a dedicated settings page.

## Main Pages
The platform includes the following main pages:

- Landing Page
- Login / Sign Up
- Dashboard
- Regulations Page
- Voting Page
- Members Page
- Settings Page
- Compliance Index Page

## Technology Stack
Sila is planned to be developed using the following technologies:

- **Frontend:** React
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
- **Backend / Database / Authentication:** Supabase
- **Language Support:** Arabic RTL
- **Fonts:** Cairo or Tajawal

## Significance of the Project
Family businesses represent an important part of the economic sector, yet many still rely on traditional and unstructured governance practices. Sila addresses this gap by introducing a digital platform that promotes better organization, stronger governance, clearer communication, and more transparent decision-making.

## Expected Outcome
Sila aims to improve governance practices in family businesses by providing a reliable digital environment that supports regulation management, structured participation, compliance monitoring, and more effective collaboration among stakeholders.
